The Sherubtse College was established in the year 1968 as a Public School, which makes it one of the oldest Colleges of The Royal University of Bhutan. It is situated at Kanglung in the district of Trashigang in east Bhutan. Besides being the oldest College of RUB, it is also the largest College. After establishing as a Public School, Sherubtse became a junior College in 1976 and then change again to become a constituent College of The Royal University of Bhutan in 2003. Now a lush green campus, Sherubtse consists of a rural flavour and offers several courses to students. The College is only 10 minutes drive from the Yongphula Airport. Schools in Sherubtse College Sherubtse College has five schools to administer different departments in the College. Each School has its own developmental feature that makes it unique from the other. read below to know more about them. School of Mathematics and Computer Science The objective of the Schools of Mathematics and Computer Science is to develop students into technocrats and IT experts, to be able to play a leading role in the information society. Moreover, students who graduate from the school of Mathematics and Computer get a variety of career opportunities, some of which includes employment the Royal Civil Services, corporation and private firms.
